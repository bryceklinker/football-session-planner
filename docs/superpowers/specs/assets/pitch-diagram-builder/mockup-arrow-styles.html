How should the three movement types look on the pitch? These are SVG paths — pick the style that reads most clearly at a glance.

Standard coaching board convention. Solid white = run, dashed blue = pass, wavy orange = dribble. Instantly readable to any coach.

High contrast with distinct colors per type. Run = thin white, pass = bold yellow, dribble = curved red. Very legible on small screens.

Single color, distinguished by thickness and dash pattern. Prints well in black & white. Simpler to implement.
